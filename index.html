My Process

01 Discover
Research & benchmarking
02 Define
Problem framing & flows
03 Design
Lo-fi → validate → hi-fi
04 Validate
Prototype & user testing
05 Deliver
Handoff & implementation

Discover

Stakeholder interviews, competitive benchmarking, and AI-assisted synthesis. I surface patterns fast — less collecting, more interpreting.

Define

Problem framing, user flows, and information architecture. Every design decision starts with a clear problem statement and a shared understanding of who we're designing for.

Design

Lo-fi wireframes to design reviews to hi-fi prototypes. I work in rapid iterations, validating assumptions at each stage rather than polishing in one long pass.

Validate

Interactive prototypes in Figma, usability testing with real users. Findings feed directly back into the design — no waiting for a final handoff to discover problems.

Deliver

Pixel-perfect specs, component documentation, and developer support through implementation. I stay close to the build — and with Claude Code, I often build alongside it.
