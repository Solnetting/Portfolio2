Overview

Unlock liquidity from your assets and earn rewards

SyntropyX is a DeFi structured vault platform that allows users to deposit BTC or other supported collateral to create personal vaults, mint synthetic assets (USX and RLX), stake them to earn rewards, and maintain control of their assets throughout.

The platform supports two core strategies — Yield-Focused and Delta-Focused — giving users fine-grained control over their risk exposure and return profile. Built at Nethermind, the protocol is designed for both experienced DeFi users and newcomers entering the on-chain economy.

How it works

01

Create a vault

Deposit BTC or other supported collateral to create your personal vault for minting synthetic assets.

02

Mint & Stake

Mint USX and RLX tokens against your collateral and stake them to earn rewards while helping secure the network.

Earn staking rewards and vault performance bonuses while maintaining control of your assets.
