Overview

Fractional access to blue-chip NFT lending

Dissect is a DeFi lending platform built on Solana that lets anyone participate in NFT-backed loans through fractionalization. Instead of needing significant capital to fund a full loan, lenders can buy fractions — getting exposure to blue-chip NFT collateral for less than 1 SOL.

I joined as Product Designer to design the marketing landing page and build the full design system from scratch. The challenge: translate a complex DeFi concept into a clear, visually compelling experience that could onboard crypto-native and crypto-curious users alike.

Core features

Five pillars of the platform

01

Loan Fractions

Fund loans by the fraction. Lenders can enter blue-chip NFT loans for less than 1 SOL, dramatically lowering the barrier to participation.

02

Portfolio Diversification

Spread lending capital across multiple loans and projects. Build a portfolio of NFT-backed positions that matches any risk appetite.

03

P2Pool Lending

Lenders pool resources together. No more bidding wars or constantly updating rates — fund once and never miss a loan opportunity again.

04

SOL Liquidity Pools

Instantly liquidate fractions from defaulted loans or purchase more to reform a whole NFT — no waiting, no friction.

05

Reform NFTs

Collect 100 fractions of the same NFT and reform them into the full asset. Missing fractions can be purchased directly through the liquidity pools.

Design System

Built from first principles

The Dissect design system was built in parallel with the product — starting with tokens, scaling up to a full component library. Every element was designed to reinforce the brand's dark, technical, premium aesthetic while remaining developer-ready.

Typography

Inter across all weights — from display/bold at 64px down to x-small/light at 12px. A 10-step type scale with paired bold and medium variants for every level, keeping hierarchy strict and legible on dark backgrounds.

Color Tokens

A token-based color system with semantic naming: surface/base, text/primary, border/focus, interactive/primary. Deep navy backgrounds with pure white text and a sharp blue accent (#3B82F6) for all interactive states.

Components

Buttons, Tabs, Landingpage cards, Field inputs, Contact links, Icon slots — every repeating element componentized in Figma with defined variants and instance-swap properties. Built for consistency across the full product surface.

Visual Language

Isometric 3D wireframe cubes as the core illustration motif — technical, geometric, and on-brand for a blockchain product. Blue radial glows anchor sections and create visual depth without competing with content.

Closing thoughts

DeFi lending

Dissect tackled one of DeFi's persistent UX failures: complexity as a moat. By designing around the fraction — the smallest unit of participation — every screen could be built to answer "what do I get?" before anything else. The design system then gave the product a premium foundation to scale from.
